Product Strategy Lead, AI Research Platform
Lyceum AI · NYC hybrid (2 days/week) or Remote (US)
Compensation: $205,000–$245,000 base + equity

About Lyceum AI
Lyceum AI (fictional) builds a platform for AI researchers and applied-ML teams to run, evaluate, and ship experiments. Our users are ML engineers and research scientists. This is a product-strategy role at the intersection of research and product.

About the role
This role owns product strategy and direction for the research platform. You'll set the roadmap, make build/buy/kill calls, define the technical product vision, and own how we evaluate model and platform tradeoffs. You'll work hand in hand with ML engineering and research leadership.

What you'll do
- Own product strategy and the roadmap for the AI research platform.
- Make core product and technical tradeoff decisions (evaluation, infrastructure, modeling).
- Define and own the product vision with research and ML engineering leaders.
- Translate frontier ML capabilities into platform direction.
- Lead a cross-functional team toward the strategy.

What we're looking for
- 8+ years in product, with deep ownership of technical / ML product strategy.
- Strong technical foundation in machine learning and model evaluation.
- Track record owning a product roadmap and making hard prioritization calls.
- Experience leading teams and setting multi-quarter strategy.
- Comfort operating with ML researchers and engineers as peers.

Lyceum AI is an equal opportunity employer. (Fictional posting for demo purposes.)
