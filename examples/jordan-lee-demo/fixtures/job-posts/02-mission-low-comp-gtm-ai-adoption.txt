About Lanternleaf Learning
Lanternleaf Learning (fictional) is a nonprofit building AI tools that help under-resourced schools adopt technology responsibly. We're mission-driven, small, and remote-first. Our comp is below market because we're a nonprofit — and we're upfront about that.

About the role
We're hiring a GTM lead to own how districts and educators discover, adopt, and succeed with our AI tools. You'll shape positioning for non-technical buyers, build adoption programs, and lead go-to-market for new releases. This is a high-impact, high-ownership role at a small organization.

What you'll do
- Own GTM and positioning for our AI-for-education products.
- Build adoption and onboarding programs for schools and educators.
- Lead launches and partner with product on what to build next.
- Run light customer research with educators and administrators.

What we're looking for
- 5+ years in product marketing or GTM, ideally with adoption / lifecycle experience.
- Ability to position technical products for non-technical buyers.
- Mission alignment and comfort in a scrappy nonprofit environment.
- Bonus: experience with AI tools or edtech.

Lanternleaf Learning is an equal opportunity employer. (Fictional posting for demo purposes.)
