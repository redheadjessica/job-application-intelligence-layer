Thornbury · Remote (US) — NYC hybrid optional

About Thornbury
Thornbury (fictional) builds an AI workflow-automation platform that helps operations and revenue teams automate multi-step work with AI agents. We're a Series B company with a strong product-marketing culture, selling to both technical and business buyers.

We're looking for a senior product marketer to own positioning, messaging, and go-to-market for our core AI workflows product. - Run customer and win-loss research; feed insights back to product and sales.
- Partner with lifecycle on adoption and activation messaging.

What we're looking for
- 6+ years in product marketing, ideally B2B SaaS / workflow or productivity tooling.
- Track record leading launches and owning positioning for technical products.
- Experience building competitive programs and running customer research.
- Pragmatic fluency using AI tools in the marketing workflow.
- Excellent, honest writing; comfortable leading cross-functionally.

Nice to have
- Experience marketing AI-native or developer-facing products.
