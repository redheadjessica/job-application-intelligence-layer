# Your cover-letter voice review — start here

I've studied your letters and writing and distilled how you sound when you're writing a letter you're proud of. **Nothing is saved yet.** This folder is for you to check my read of your voice before it starts writing letters.

## How to review (the easy way)

- Open the files **in order**, 1 → 6.
- The one question that matters everywhere: **"Do I actually sound like this?"**
- Easiest feedback: **talk it back to me in chat** (voice-to-text is great) — you don't have to edit anything.
- You can also **edit these files directly**. If you do, **tell me when you're done** and I'll reread them before saving.
- **Nothing is promoted until you say it's good.**

## What's in this folder

1. **Voice Spec** — the rules a letter in your voice follows, with evidence from your own letters.
2. **Exemplars** — the real letters I propose as your reference set (one GOLD standard).
3. **Anecdote Bank** — your true stories, indexed for reuse. Check every fact.
4. **Writing Links** — your public work and when to link each piece. Check every URL.
5. **Config + Formatting** — your signature, the .docx styling, and the lint knobs.
6. **Open Questions** — what I still need from you.

When you're ready, tell me: **"this sounds like me"** — or tell me what's off.
