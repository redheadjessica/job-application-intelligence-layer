## The truth firewall (why this matters)

Anything from your **`02-where-you-want-to-go/`** materials shapes your **direction, scoring, and target lanes** — it is **never** treated as proof you've done that work. If you see a target role being written up as your experience anywhere below, flag it. That's the one mistake this whole system is built to prevent.
