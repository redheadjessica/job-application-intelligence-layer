## Compensation-Question Behavior
When you **deliberately include a role in an application batch, assume the stated compensation is acceptable.** Do not repeatedly ask whether the salary is high enough or treat compensation as an application gate unless:
- the salary is missing
- there is a hidden or contradictory compensation issue
