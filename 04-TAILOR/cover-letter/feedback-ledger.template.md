# Cover Letter Feedback Ledger

Your accumulated feedback on cover-letter writing. **The writer and evaluator read this every
run. Newest entries win over older entries AND over `voice-spec.md` when they conflict.**

How entries get here (church-and-state rule):
- You say something directly in a session → recorded here immediately.
- The post-submission reconcile pass (diffing the agent's baseline against the PDF you actually
  submitted) proposes **candidate** lessons into `feedback-queue.md`. Only after you confirm
  ("yes, that was on purpose — make it a rule") does an entry get promoted here.
- Never write an entry here based on an unconfirmed inference.

Entry format: date, the rule in plain English, and (when useful) the example that produced it.
