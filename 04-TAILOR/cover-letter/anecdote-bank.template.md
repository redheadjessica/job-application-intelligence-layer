# Anecdote Bank — your true stories, indexed for reuse

Personal stories and lived details for cover letters and application answers. **This bank (plus the
application canon and you directly) is the ONLY permitted source of personal stories** — the
writer never invents, embellishes, or composites. Each entry is tagged with its source.

**Usage rules for the writer:**
- Check **Used in** before reusing: never send the same story to the same company twice, and vary
  stories across a company's multiple roles.
- Match on **Themes / Use when**, not surface keywords. One or two sentences of story is the dose.
- Stories may be lightly re-angled for the role but never changed factually.
- The reconcile pass appends new entries and updates **Used in** — keep its formatting.

Entry format: heading = slug · Status/source · The story · Themes · Use when · Used in · Guardrails.
