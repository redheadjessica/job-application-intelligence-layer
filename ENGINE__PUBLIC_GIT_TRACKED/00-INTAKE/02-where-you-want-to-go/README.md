# 02 - Where You Want To Go  (direction)

Put materials here that describe **where you're headed** — the roles, companies, and kinds of work you're aiming for. Intake uses these to shape your scoring rubric, your priority lanes, and what to look for in a job batch.

**Good things to add:**
- Job descriptions for roles you genuinely want (dream jobs, target-company roles).
- Roles you're curious about but not sure you qualify for yet.
- "I want more of this" / "I don't want this anymore" examples.
- Target job titles, industries, and company types.
- Workstyle, comp, and location preferences (or just tell intake in chat).

**The one rule — the truth firewall:** these materials shape **direction and scoring only.** They are **never** treated as proof that you've done this work. Wanting a role does not put its requirements on your resume.

If something here is actually a role you *held*, it's evidence — move it to `../01-about-you/` instead.

Everything in this folder stays local and is gitignored — it is never committed.
