# 01 - About You  (evidence)

Put materials here that are **evidence about you** — the real record of what you've actually done. Intake uses these to build your profile and experience bank.

**Good things to add:**
- Your current resume, plus older or rough versions (more is better — intake reconciles them).
- A LinkedIn export (on your profile: **More → Save to PDF**, or **Settings → Get a copy of your data**) or screenshots.
- Brag/wins docs, accomplishments, performance reviews, promotion packets, self-reviews, manager/peer feedback.
- Real metrics and numbers you can defend (team size, growth, conversion, retention, etc.).
- Project docs, launch notes, case studies, screenshots of shipped work.
- Writing samples, a portfolio, or published work (used for your voice and credibility).
- **Job descriptions for roles you have actually held** — a past or current job's posting describes your real work in an employer's words.

**The one rule:** a job description belongs here **only if you actually held that role.** A posting for a job you *want* is not evidence about you — that goes in `../02-where-you-want-to-go/`.

Everything in this folder stays local and is gitignored — it is never committed.
