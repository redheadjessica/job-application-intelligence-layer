# Intake — set the pipeline up for yourself

Run **`/intake`** once before vetting or tailoring. It reads your career materials, gives you an honest
read on them, asks a few questions, and generates your personalized files so the rest of the pipeline
works for you.

## Drop your materials in `your-materials/`

**Required:** at least one resume — `.pdf`, `.docx`, `.txt`, or `.md`.
(Apple Pages? Export to PDF first, or paste the text when asked.)

**Optional, but more signal = a better setup:**
- Other resume versions (old or rough is fine)
- LinkedIn (export, or paste your About + experience)
- Past cover letters
- A brag / wins doc
- Performance reviews
- Writing samples
- Job descriptions of roles you *want* (they reveal direction)

Everything you drop here stays local: `your-materials/` and the generated `resume-assessment.md` are
gitignored, so your personal data is never committed.

## Then run `/intake`

It works in two tiers — **Tier 1** gets you ranking jobs in ~5 minutes; **Tier 2** goes deeper so it can
draft tailored resumes. You can stop after Tier 1 and come back.

It will be honest with you about your materials. That's the point — it's here to help you get hired, not
to flatter you.
